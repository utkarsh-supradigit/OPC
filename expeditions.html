Expedition 1: An Odyssey of Adventure

Dates: November 2022 - April, 2023 (Completed)
The acquisition of GREGAL was motivated by OPC’s broader social objectives. While the boat’s immediate purpose was to support Abhilash’s participation in GGR, its long-term vision was focused on pioneering HKC Expeditions. The objective: to leverage GREGAL for inspiring sea-based adventures aimed at exploration, community engagement, and fostering maritime heritage.

Expedition 2: A Journey of Discovery

Dates: February 21, 2024 - March 20, 2024 (Completed)

Expedition 2 marked HKC’s first land-based journey, beginning in Mumbai and coinciding with the birthday of The Mother, co-founder of Auroville, Pondicherry. This expedition brought together a group of five friends from Normandy, France, who joined the journey to explore the diverse cultural and spiritual landscapes of India.

Expedition 3: A Spiritual Sojourn Through South India

Dates: August 15, 2024 – August 30, 2024 (Completed)
Expedition 03 marked the continuation of our journey, building upon the momentum of Expedition 02 that began in Pondicherry. We set sail on August 15, 2024, a date rich with significance for India— marking both the country’s Independence Day and the birthday of Sri Aurobindo, the founder and spiritual leader of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram in Pondicherry.
